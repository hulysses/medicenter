Fluctuating blood pressure could influence cognitive decline risk

A new study has found that people with high blood pressure who take all their anti-hypertensive medications in one go at bedtime have better controlled blood pressure and a significantly lower risk of death or illness caused by heart or blood vessel problems.

Can tattoos cause blood or skin cancer?

A new study has found that people with tattoos have a higher risk of skin cancer than those without tattoos. This risk is particularly high for people with black or red tattoos, and those who have had a tattoo for a long time.

What is dry eye and how can I get rid of it?

Dry eye occurs when the eyes produce too few tears or the tears evaporate too fast. The condition can affect one or both eyes. Lifestyle changes can help, but in severe cases, a person may need medication or surgery.

How do COVID-19 vaccines work?

COVID-19 vaccines work by introducing the immune system to an inactivated form of the SARS-CoV-2 coronavirus or a part of it. This does not cause COVID-19 but equips the body to fight against future infection with the virus.
